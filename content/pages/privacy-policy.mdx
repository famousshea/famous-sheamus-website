## How does Famous Sheamus protect my business data?

We operate fundamentally differently from standard SaaS providers. Because our core philosophy is "No New Software," we do not host, store, or warehouse your customer data. Instead, we architect solutions using tools like n8n and Vapi that live within your own infrastructure or operate in strict "Zero-Retention" mode. This means data passes through our automated workflows to execute a task but is never saved or used to train external or public Generative AI models.

## What AI technologies do you use, and how are they transparent?

In accordance with the Texas Responsible AI Governance Act (TRAIGA), we are fully transparent about our use of artificial intelligence. We deploy **Agentic AI** exclusively for lead capture and workflow orchestration. Additionally, we utilize **Voice AI** to handle automated appointment scheduling. In every instance where an individual interacts with our Voice AI or Agentic AI systems, they will be clearly notified that they are speaking with an automated assistant.

## Do you comply with TCPA and automated voice regulations?

Yes. Before any automated voice interaction is initiated via our Voice AI systems, we require "Prior Express Written Consent" as mandated by the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA). Our architectures are designed to ensure your business captures and records this consent securely before any outbound AI call is triggered.

## What are my rights under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA)?

As a Texas-based consultancy, we strictly adhere to the TDPSA. You have the right to confirm whether we are processing your personal data, the right to access that data, and the right to request its deletion. Because we process data in your own stack, any requests for data deletion from our temporary diagnostic logs are honored immediately. We do not sell personal data, nor do we process it for targeted advertising without explicit consent.
