## What is the scope of the Fractional CTO role?

Famous Sheamus Consulting operates primarily in a strategic and architectural capacity. The "Fractional CTO" role is designed to eliminate operational drag by orchestrating your existing software (e.g., CRMs, ERPs) through our "No New Software" philosophy. We build AI automation blueprints, oversee technical direction, and optimize workflows. While our standard engagement model focuses on architecture, we offer full IT custody and managed systems for qualifying clients. These expanded services are governed by separate, private operating agreements.

## Who owns the software and data?

You do. We do not build proprietary SaaS platforms for you to lease. Our automations are built using platforms like n8n and Vapi that integrate directly into your existing infrastructure. Because the software remains yours, you retain all ownership, control, and responsibility for the data flowing through it. We configure these systems to operate in "Zero-Retention" mode to ensure your business data is never used to train public AI models. 

## What are the responsibilities regarding AI Transparency and TRAIGA?

Under the Texas Responsible AI Governance Act (TRAIGA), businesses must be transparent about the use of AI. When we architect **Agentic AI** systems for lead capture or **Voice AI** for appointment scheduling, we configure the systems to explicitly disclose their automated nature to the end-user. As the client and owner of the deployed systems, you agree to maintain these transparency disclosures and not alter the workflows to deceive consumers.

## How do we handle TCPA compliance for Voice AI?

Our Voice AI architectures are powerful tools for scaling operations, but they carry strict legal responsibilities. You, the client, are solely responsible for ensuring that you have obtained "Prior Express Written Consent" from any individual before our automated Voice AI systems initiate an outbound call or text to them, in strict compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and relevant local laws. Famous Sheamus Consulting provides the architecture, but the liability for consent acquisition rests entirely with your organization.
