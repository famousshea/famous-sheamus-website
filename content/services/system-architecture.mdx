## The Problem

Your business has outgrown its technology. The MVP that got you to this point — cobbled together from spreadsheets, shared drives, and disconnected SaaS tools — is now actively slowing you down. Performance is inconsistent, security is an afterthought, and every new feature or integration feels like it could break something.

Development agencies want to build custom software (adding cost and complexity). Corporate consultants deliver strategy decks with no execution plan. Neither addresses the root issue: **your technology infrastructure wasn't designed to scale.**

## The Solution

As a Fractional CTO, I design technical infrastructure that **decouples your revenue from your headcount.** Instead of hiring more engineers to manage complexity, I architect systems where complexity is managed by design.

**What a system architecture engagement covers:**

- **Cloud Infrastructure Strategy** — Selecting and configuring the right cloud services (AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure) based on your actual needs — not vendor lock-in.
- **API Architecture** — Designing clean, versioned APIs that allow your internal tools, customer-facing apps, and third-party integrations to communicate reliably.
- **Database Strategy** — Choosing the right database technology (relational, document, or graph) and designing schemas that support your query patterns at scale.
- **Security Architecture** — Implementing zero-trust principles, encryption at rest and in transit, and access control patterns that meet compliance requirements (HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI-DSS).
- **Monitoring & Observability** — Building dashboards and alerting systems so you know exactly what's happening in your infrastructure before problems become outages.

**How this differs from a development agency:** I work at the **infrastructure layer** — the part of the stack that determines whether features perform at scale or collapse under load. Using battle-tested, open-source, and commodity cloud services rather than proprietary platforms that create vendor lock-in.

## The Outcome

- **A production-grade technical blueprint** with architecture diagrams, technology recommendations, and a phased implementation roadmap.
- **Scalable infrastructure** that supports your growth trajectory without requiring constant re-engineering.
- **Compliance-ready architecture** designed with security and audit requirements built in from day one.
- **Full knowledge transfer** — your team receives complete documentation so you're never dependent on a single consultant.
