## The Problem

Your business is leaking leads from every channel. Website visitors leave without converting. After-hours phone calls go to voicemail. Contact form submissions sit unread for 48 hours. Social media DMs get buried in notifications.

Research shows that responding to a lead within 5 minutes makes you **21x more likely to qualify them** compared to responding in 30 minutes. Most small businesses take hours — or days. Every hour of delay is revenue walking to a competitor with faster follow-up.

## The Solution

AI-Powered Lead Capture creates a **unified, always-on lead qualification system** across every channel your prospects use — phone, web, email, SMS, and social media.

**How the system works:**

- **Website:** Intelligent chat widgets engage visitors, answer initial questions, and capture contact details before they leave. No generic "How can I help you?" — the widget asks qualifying questions based on your actual service offerings.
- **Phone:** Voice AI receptionist answers calls 24/7, qualifies the lead based on your business rules, and books appointments directly into your calendar or CRM.
- **Email & SMS:** Automated response sequences engage new leads within seconds of form submission, keeping them warm until your team follows up.
- **Social Media:** DM monitoring and automated responses on Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn capture leads from platforms your team doesn't have time to monitor.

**Lead scoring and routing:**

Every captured lead is automatically scored based on criteria you define — service area, job type, budget, urgency. High-value leads are routed immediately to your sales team with full context. Lower-priority leads enter a nurture sequence until they're ready to convert.

## The Outcome

- **Zero missed leads.** Every inquiry across every channel gets an immediate, intelligent response — 24/7/365.
- **Sub-5-minute response times** on every channel, dramatically improving qualification rates.
- **Lead data flows directly into your CRM** — no manual entry, no lost spreadsheets, no dropped follow-ups.
- **Clear attribution.** Dashboard metrics show exactly which channels are generating qualified leads and which need optimization.

### Who is this for?

- Home service businesses where a single captured lead can be worth $500–$5,000 in revenue.
- Professional service firms (law, accounting, consulting) losing prospects to competitors with faster response times.
- Any business generating leads from more than two channels without a unified system to capture and qualify them.
