## The Problem

Your team is "always busy" but growth has stalled. Everyone is working hard, yet revenue isn't scaling proportionally. The reason is **operational drag** — the invisible friction created by manual handoffs, redundant data entry, disconnected tools, and processes that nobody has questioned in years.

Most businesses don't know where the drag is worst. They know automation "could help" but can't quantify where to start or what return to expect. Guessing leads to wasted investment in the wrong tools.

## The Solution

Process Analysis & Optimization is the **audit phase that precedes any automation work.** I conduct a structured, data-driven analysis of how your business actually operates — not how you think it operates — to reveal the hidden inefficiencies that are quietly draining your margins.

**What the audit delivers:**

- **Workflow Mapping** — Documenting every step of your core business processes, from lead capture through service delivery to invoicing and follow-up.
- **Time & Cost Analysis** — Quantifying how many hours per week your team spends on each manual task and calculating the real dollar cost of that labor.
- **Bottleneck Identification** — Pinpointing where work stalls, where data gets re-entered, and where handoffs between people or systems create delays.
- **Tool Audit** — Evaluating your current software stack for overlap, underutilization, and SaaS bloat. You may be paying for 5 tools when 2 would do.
- **Automation Opportunity Scoring** — Ranking every identified inefficiency by impact (time saved) and feasibility (complexity to automate), so we tackle high-ROI wins first.

**What you receive at the end:**

1. **Current State Map** — A visual diagram of your existing workflows with pain points highlighted.
2. **Efficiency Scorecard** — A breakdown of time and cost waste by department and process.
3. **Automation Roadmap** — A prioritized list of automation opportunities ranked by ROI with estimated implementation timelines.
4. **Quick Wins Playbook** — Immediate, low-effort changes that start saving time within the first week.

## The Outcome

- **15–30 hours per week of manual work identified as automatable** in a typical engagement.
- **$19,500–$39,000 per year in recovered productivity** for a team of 10, before counting revenue impact.
- **A clear, prioritized roadmap** so you invest in the automation that delivers the highest return first.
- **No guesswork.** Every recommendation is backed by data from your actual operations.
