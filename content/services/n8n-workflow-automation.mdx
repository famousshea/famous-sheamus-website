## The Problem

Your team spends more time copying data between systems, sending follow-up emails, and updating spreadsheets than doing the work that actually generates revenue. This is **operational drag** — and it scales linearly with headcount. Every new hire inherits the same manual processes, and the problem compounds.

Meanwhile, the automation platforms promising to fix this (Zapier, Make.com) charge per task. As your operations scale, your monthly automation bill explodes — a mid-size team can easily spend $500–$2,000/month and still lack the complex, multi-step workflows their business actually needs.

## The Solution

Using **self-hosted N8N**, I build custom multi-step automation workflows that connect your existing tools, synchronize your data, and execute complex business logic — all running silently in the background, 24/7.

**Why self-hosted N8N instead of SaaS automation platforms:**

- **No per-task pricing.** Run 10 workflows or 10,000 — same cost.
- **Complete data sovereignty.** Customer data, API keys, and business logic never leave your infrastructure.
- **Unlimited customization.** Custom code nodes, direct database access, and complex branching logic that SaaS platforms cannot support.
- **No vendor lock-in.** Your workflows are portable and version-controlled.
