## What tools do you work with for automation?

Using N8N, I synchronize data between hundreds of applications, from Google Workspace and Microsoft 365, to specialized CRMs, accounting tools like QuickBooks, and internal databases. If it has an API, we can automate it.

### Why choose self-hosted N8N over Zapier or Make?

Zapier charges premium prices per "zap" or per task. As your operations scale, your fixed costs explode. By using self-hosted N8N, Famous Sheamus Consulting ensures your automation workflow scales without increasing monthly task costs. Your data remains completely under your control, ensuring maximum intellectual property protection.

## Can these workflows handle social media?

Absolutely. We build custom Social Media Engines that autonomously craft and post high-quality content directly to LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook. It’s not just posting—it’s intelligent interaction, engaging with industry trends automatically.
