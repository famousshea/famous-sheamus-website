## The Problem

**Missed calls are missed money.** In home services, the average missed call represents $200–$1,200 in lost revenue. After-hours inquiries, weekend emergencies, and high-volume seasonal surges all create the same problem: your phone rings and nobody answers. Your technicians are in the field, your admin is overwhelmed, and the lead goes to a competitor who picked up.

A human dispatcher costs $35,000–$55,000/year. The $49/month AI receptionist alternatives on the market answer the phone — but the data sits in a silo. Customer details never make it into your CRM without manual entry, violating the entire purpose of automation.

## The Solution

Our Voice AI Receptionist provides **24/7 coverage with deep CRM integration** — answering every call, qualifying every lead, and booking appointments directly into your existing scheduling software. No new apps. No manual data entry. No missed revenue.

**What the AI handles:**

- **Inbound call answering** with natural, conversational AI that sounds human — not robotic.
- **Lead qualification** based on your specific business rules (service area, job type, urgency level).
- **Appointment scheduling** directly into Jobber, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, or your CRM of choice.
- **Solicitor screening** to filter out spam, robocalls, and sales pitches.
- **Emergency escalation** with configurable rules to route urgent calls to on-call technicians.
- **Call transcription and summarization** attached to the customer record automatically.

**Why latency matters:** Most off-the-shelf voice bots stack speech-to-text, LLM inference, and text-to-speech sequentially — creating pauses that feel unnatural and cause hang-ups. Our architecture delivers **sub-500ms response times** through streaming speech-to-text, intent-aware caching, and edge-deployed inference.
