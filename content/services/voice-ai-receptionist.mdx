## What types of tasks can this AI Receptionist automate?

Our Voice AI Receptionist completely removes the burden of administrative call management from your technicians. Whether you're a plumber, electrician, or HVAC professional, the receptionist handles initial contact, screens out solicitors, and intelligently schedules appointments based on calendar availability.

### How does the integration with Jobber or ServiceTitan work?

We establish a secure API connection between our AI Voice agent and your existing CRMs like Jobber or ServiceTitan. Without you lifting a finger, customer details, call transcripts, and appointment block reservations are logged directly into the software you already use.

## How much does automation typically cost?

A self-hosted voice AI agent is a fraction of the cost of hiring a 24/7 human dispatcher. Due to our custom integrations, we charge an upfront setup fee plus a low, predictable monthly maintenance rate based on call volume, completely eliminating high hourly wages and unexpected overtime.
