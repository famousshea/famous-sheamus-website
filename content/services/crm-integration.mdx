## The Problem

The average small business uses 12–15 SaaS tools. In most cases, these tools don't talk to each other. Customer data lives in your CRM. Invoices live in your accounting software. Job details live in your dispatch platform. Communications live in email and Slack.

The result is **data fragmentation.** Your team spends hours every day manually copying information between systems, and critical details fall through the cracks. A lead captured by your Voice AI never makes it into your CRM. An invoice gets generated but the customer record isn't updated. A job is completed but the follow-up review request is never sent.

## The Solution

I build **automated, real-time data bridges** between every system your business depends on — eliminating manual data entry and ensuring that every team works from one source of truth.

**Common integration scenarios:**

| Source System | Destination System | Data Flow |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Voice AI / Web Forms | Jobber, ServiceTitan, HubSpot | Lead → CRM contact + job creation |
| CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce) | QuickBooks, Xero | Won deal → automatic invoice generation |
| Dispatch (Jobber, ServiceTitan) | Google Calendar, Outlook | Job schedule → technician calendar sync |
| Stripe / Square | Accounting + CRM | Payment → reconciliation + status update |
| Project management (Jira, Linear) | Slack, Teams | Status change → team notification |

**How this differs from Zapier:** Real business data flows are rarely simple triggers. Our integrations handle complex conditional logic, data transformation and deduplication, bi-directional sync, error handling with automatic retry, and high-volume throughput — all without per-task pricing.

## The Outcome

- **Zero manual data entry** between systems. Information flows automatically and in real time.
- **One source of truth.** Every team sees the same, up-to-date customer and project data regardless of which tool they use.
- **Errors and dropped data eliminated.** Automated error handling and retry logic ensure nothing falls through the cracks.
- **Full monitoring.** Real-time dashboards show data flow health, error rates, and throughput.
