## Why does a small business need a Fractional CTO?

Most small businesses hit a technical ceiling where off-the-shelf software and Zapier integrations turn into a tangled, unreliable mess. At this stage, founders spend their time putting out operational fires instead of scaling revenue. A Fractional CTO audits your entire technical stack and designs custom, resilient architecture. They implement intelligent automation and AI solutions that seamlessly integrate with your existing workforce, effectively "buying back your time."
